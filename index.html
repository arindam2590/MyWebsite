About Me

I am a Ph.D. research scholar at IIIT Allahabad, specializing in Multi-Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning with a strong passion for Artificial Intelligence, Swarm Robotics, and Collective Intelligence in Multi Agent Systems. My research focuses on designing intelligent, decentralized multi agent systems for real-world applications such as search and rescue missions, defense automation, and adaptive path planning.

With a solid foundation in machine learning, multi-agent, and swarm simulation, I aim to bridge theoretical advances with impactful solutions for national development. I am deeply motivated to contribute to cutting-edge research that strengthens the technological backbone of our country.
